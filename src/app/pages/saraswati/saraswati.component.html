NVBA Rangamancha

This year, we have curated a lively Saraswati Puja celebration brimming with devotional rituals, cultural performances, and engaging activities suitable for all ages. You can look forward to captivating performances by our talented local artists, showcasing a diverse range of songs, dances, recitations, and instrumental pieces.

Enhancing the cultural tapestry of our event, we're excited to introduce a captivating dance drama "Kaljoyi Rabindranath" by Ta Thoi. Inspired by three of Tagore's acclaimed works (Chandalika, Chitrangada, and Tasher Desh), each resonating with significant social themes, this performance promises to be a highlight of our local cultural segment.

As the evening unfolds, prepare to be mesmerized by the electrifying performance of Band Imon from New York, adding an extra spark of excitement to our festivities. Imon focuses on a varied range of music starting from Indian classical, to Bollywood, to Western Pop, and Rock songs, occasionally fusing different musical forms to bring the best of both worlds.
